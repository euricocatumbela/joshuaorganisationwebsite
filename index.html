About our Company

Joshua Organisation (Pty) is a company based in Cape Town whose task is to provide support in Maritime Training, Education, Transportation, Accommodation and Tourism services. We connect you to the best maritime training solutions which enfold all facets of Human Resource Development and Consultancy. All our partner-providers are SAMSA (STCW) accredited, and many of them also have OPITO accreditation for some of the offshore course.

Member of Cape Chamber of Commerce & Industry, we undertake and specialize in Student Management whereby we facilitate the entire process of student enrolment in reliable, accredited institutions which meet all the international standards of IMO and STCW Convention and OPITO as well as South African Maritime Safety Authority (SAMSA) and Local Authorities.

A strong, customer-focused approach, conformance to high business standards and the continual quest for top-class quality service has enabled the company to sustain effectiveness in the chosen industries of which Joshua Organisation has been firmly structured upon. Thus in turn making the information the company has access to, valuable to any client seeking our assistance through the services we deliver.

The company maintains a solid reputation for the expertise of its consulting and responsiveness of the support team altogether. The team has been trained to keep pace with developments within the operating industries as well as the emerging needs of its clients. The company’s service offerings are individually tailored to meet the needs of each client therefore growing the business solely through serving the needs of the client.

Gansbaai near Cape Town is one of the best spots in the world to watch the Great White Sharks in action and at CapeTownAttractions, we pride ourselves as we provide the very best fun diving trips. We invite you to discover all the underwater beauties, the Cape has to offer, especially cage diving with the giants of the ocean - The Great White Sharks. Plunge in water in a custom built shark cage and dive with these impressive creatures for around 15 minutes. What is interesting is that no previous diving experience is needed and rest assured as our shark cage diving trips are done with topmost safety.

The Cape Town Safari Tour is a fantastic way to see African wildlife in its natural setting without trekking to far-off corners of the continent. It is ideal for families with kids. Before arriving at the Inverdoorn Game Reserve, go through the inland to discover other aspects of the Western Cape. During this two and a half hour drive, you will be glad to be able to just look out the window at the beautiful scenery displaying all the diversity of South Africa.

There is something magical about the Cape Winelands. Steeped in history and offering magnificent scenery and award-winning wines, exploring the Winelands is the perfect way to spend a day. There is nothing better than sitting outside, under the shady branches of an obliging tree, sipping Cab Sav while gazing out over rolling hills adorned with vivid red-gold vines. Imagine a day spent hopping between three of the Cape’s most celebrated wine routes, indulging in a visual and culinary feast. Stellenbosch, Franschhoek and Paarl are some of South Africa’s most renowned destinations, known for their phenomenal scenery and quaint charm, with leaf lined avenues and classic Cape Dutch architecture.

The five-minute ascent to the top of Table Mountain offers a 360° view of Cape Town, the ocean and the neighbouring peaks. Once at the top visitors enjoy spectacular views and the peaceful feeling of being over one thousand metres above the city below. The area at the top of the mountain is surprisingly large so leave enough time to stroll along the paths, enjoy the viewing platforms, and soak up the vistas.

As with our vineyards, you’ll find that people in Noordhoek likewise take it slow. So too in the kitchen we take time in selecting fresh ingredients to compliment our Sauvignon Blanc driven wines. So whilst you are on this side of the mountain, we ask you to succumb to our nature: Take your time, sit back, relax, and simply enjoy life and the breathtaking view.
